MY WATCH IS BROKEN!


This project was the result of a collaboration between me and Giulia Frumento. It took part to a contest for a special edition of Ziggi Urs rolling papers inspired to the song 4:20 of the Italian rapper Ensi.
The copy 420 - My watch is broken! was inspired by the chorus of the song: il mio orologio è rotto e fermo sulle 4:20 (in english my watch is broken and stopped on 4:20).

Illustrations were made by Giulia while i developed the concept and worked on the background style, on the details and on the typeface.
